Programmer's Magic 8-Ball

A simple app that give a random response when it is shaken.

Download .zip Download .tar.gz View on GitHub

Built in Xcode 7.2 for Swift 2.0

A magic 8-ball for programmers.

Have you ever been stuck on a coding problem, banging your head, and wish you could shake a magical 8-ball and it would just give you the right answer?

We can't do that, but we can give you a random solution instead. This magic 8-ball app is packed with tons of possible solutions, humorous jokes, and sly insults, to hopefully help find the solution to your programming problem. Tell it your problem and shake your phone and we'll give you a response.

The Magic 8-Ball is not responsible for your programming decisions
